Tactics Thrill

About


    For new chess players, it can be hard to understand the importance of practicing tactics, and particularily young players can have a hard time focusing on a sometimes boring task. Secondarily, young players are often too focused on their tactics rating to take chances against ruining their statistics. This iOS and Android does away with the ratings, but also presents tactics in a much more ingaging and fun way. We call this gamemode 'Blitz,' but we do have two other more relaxing gamemodes for training your chess vision ('Levels' and 'Endless'). Created with React Native (Javascript), the app started production in November 2018 and the first design was finished by Janurary 2019. When we started the project in November, 'Blitz' was an original idea, but apparently we weren't the only ones to think of it. Chess.com (the most popular platform for online chess) released a similar game called 'Puzzle Rush' just a month into our development.
