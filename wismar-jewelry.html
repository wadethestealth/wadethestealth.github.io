Project

Wismar Jewelry

About


     Without a way to look up and categorize their products, Wismar Jewelry had to manually remember what they had in stock, which can be difficult for a custom jeweler, or spend minutes looking in their catelogs to find any particular piece they were looking for. The solution I installed was an internal browser-based tool that not only adhered to modern design rules, but also gave the business a way to categorize and easily search through their products. This was my first project made for an official business, and it used of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This was created in June 2018 and the final design was finished by August 2018.
